Hockey Ranked (“we,” “our,” or “the Bot”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect
	•	User-Provided Information: Your Discord username, user ID, and any data you provide to the bot (e.g., game scores, team selections, or rank-related inputs).
	•	Automatically Collected Information: Messages or interactions with the bot, timestamps of commands, and server ID for ranking purposes.
	•	Non-Personal Analytics: Aggregated statistics about bot usage and rankings.

2. How We Use Your Data
	•	Track player ranks and leaderboard positions.
	•	Display statistics and rankings within the bot.
	•	Prevent abuse or cheating in rankings.
	•	Improve and maintain bot functionality.
	•	Analyze usage trends for bot improvements.

3. Data Sharing
	•	We do not sell or rent your personal information.
	•	Data may be shared with trusted third-party services (e.g., hosting providers) to operate the bot.
	•	Your data may be disclosed if required by law or to enforce Terms & Conditions.

4. Data Retention
	•	Player rankings and relevant data are stored until the user requests deletion or the bot is decommissioned.
	•	Aggregated analytics may be retained for bot improvement purposes.

5. User Rights
	•	You may request deletion of your data by contacting [insert contact email or Discord].
	•	You can revoke bot access from your server, which will stop the collection of new data.

6. Security
	•	We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

7. Changes to This Policy
	•	We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available on this site.
